The Educational Website

Welcome

The Educational Website is a web page made for and by international students all across the globe. On this website you're able to enhance your abilities in multiple different subjects by studying them in a manner that allows you to stay on track and stay interested!

Who are we?

Briefly, we're a group of students (just like you) who are trying to simplify learning and make studying fun. If you would like to contribute to the project visit the Contribute page.

How often do we update the website?

We update the website with improvements and new content all the time! With the ability for other users to contribute to the project we can easily accept their additions and add them very easily and very fast.

Last Update: 12th of July 2015

Is our information reliable?

All the content is double-checked from at least two sources that confirm its legitimacy. We also always include sources at the end of each document so if you have doubts on validity, you can check.
